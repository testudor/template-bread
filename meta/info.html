This template is part of the "modular breadboard" parent project. It includes PCB edge, mounting holes and keepout zones required to design your very own breadboard module.
The filled zones on the top and bottom layer are optional, everything else is not. The keepout zone prevents the bottom copper layer from making a connection to the metal base plate should the solder mask wear through.
